How to use

From generating to sharing — everything you need to know.

Generating a QR code
1

Enter network details

Type your SSID (network name) and password. The password is encoded silently — it never appears on screen for guests.

2

Customize and preview

Choose a QR size, color, and error-correction level. The preview updates live as you type.

3

Download or save

Download as a PNG to print and display. Hit Save to keep the network in your local list for quick access later.

Scanning on Android
1

Open Camera

Use the default Camera app or Google Lens on Android 10 and above.

2

Point and tap

Point at the QR code. A banner appears — tap it. Android connects automatically with no password entry.

Older Android: install a WiFi QR scanner app from the Play Store.

Scanning on iPhone / iPad
1

Open Camera app

Requires iOS 11 or later. No third-party app needed.

2

Point and tap the banner

Tap the notification banner at the top of the screen. iPhone joins the network immediately.

Privacy & security notes
  • All QR generation runs entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to any server.
  • Saved networks are stored in your browser's localStorage — clearing browser data deletes them.
  • Anyone who scans the QR can join the network. To revoke access, change your WiFi password.
  • For guests, consider using a separate guest network with its own password.
  • The QR format used is the open standard: WIFI:T:WPA;S:name;P:pass;;
